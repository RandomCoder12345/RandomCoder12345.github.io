Samus

She may have lost her Super Suit, but she still has exceptional speed. She comes with a paralyzer, a whip, and her rocket shoes to pull off sick combos.

One of the newer characters to be added to Ultimate, Ridley comes from a sharp long tail and sharp claws. Players can use these to cause devastating damage to opponents.
