Fire Emblem

Marth

This exceptional swordfighter has a long reach so most of his damage will come frim attacks using the tip of his sword. With this in mind, make sure when you use this character, you keep the distance between you and the opponent in mind.

Lucina

Lucina, female marth, shares that same attacks as marth. Lucina is more powerful than Marth as she can do quite a bit of damage anywhere on her sword.

Roy

Beware of his flames! The closer you are to Roy the more you are going to regret it.

Chrom

This is Roy's echo fighter but without any of the flames on his sword.

Ike

Ike's attacks are very powerful and del lots of damage but they also leave himwide open for attacks. Make sure to read your opponents movements, air dodges, or rolls to make sure you time those smash attacks.

Robin

This fighter weilds his Levin Sword and his four different Times. These weapons can only be used a certain number of times, so be sure you use them wisely.

Corrin

This is a unique fighter. This character uses Torrential Roar and Dragon Fang Shot. This makes her effective in both close and long range.
