Kirby

The Hero of Dreamland comes to the stage for a treat. Kirby has the ability to copy other fighters and has a flutter jump to make a great recovery. His down special turns him into a rock to smash his enemies.

Meta Knight

The most broken character in Brawl has come back more balanced. Meta Knight is filled with quick moves, midair jumps, and counters.

King Dedede

This beefy boy is a heavyweight fighter with a great recovery. His move, hovering, allows him to float back on stage like Kirby.
