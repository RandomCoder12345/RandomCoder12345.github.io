Masahiro Sakurai

Masahiro Sakurai was born on August 3,1970. He is currently a Japanese fame developer, creator, writer, and voice actor. He is known for creating Kirby and the Super Smsh Bros. series, both he developed during his tenur in HAL Labroratory. Currently, he is working as the director of the Super Smash Bros. series through his company Sora Ltd.

Super Smash Bros. History

Super Smash Bros. is a crossover fighting game that was made for various consoles. The first release was in 1999 for the Nintendo 64. Then followed by Super Smash Bros. Melee in 2001-2002 for the Gamecube. After Melee came Super Smash Bros. Brawl in 2008 for the Wii. After many Metaknight mains later, Nintendo released Super Smash Bros. for the 3Ds and Wii U in 2014. 51 Zero tournament wins later, Nintendo released the current version, Super Smash Bros. for the Nintendo Switch in 2018.
