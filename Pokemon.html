Pokemon

Pikachu

This fiesty pokemon uses electric moves such as thunder bold and volt tackle to shock enemies to defeat.

Jigglypuff

Jigglypuff can use its great aerial ability to overwhelm your opponent. If you can land rest on an opponent, it will launch them away.

Pichu

Pichu and Pikachu have similar movesets but Pichu is a little weaker and takes damage when he does electric attacks.

Mewtwo

This legendary pokemon has technical moves such as confusion or disable as diversions. It has projectiles such as its shadowball.

Pokemon Trainer (Squirtle, Ivysaur, Charizard)

Pokemon Trainer's can choose from Squirtle, Ivysaur, or Charizard to deal damage or recover.

Greninja

This ninja-like pokemon uses his water shurkiens and his stealth to maneuver and counter opponents attaks.

Lucario

Lucario attack power increases as it takes damage. This makes Lucario a very dangerous opponent to face since the more damage you do, the stronger he gets.

Incineroar

This massive tank can definetly tank hits. Using this ability, Incineroar can send attacks back at the opponent with the same amount of damage.
