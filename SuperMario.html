Super Mario

Mario

The Hero of the Mushroom Kingdom has a wide variety of techniques to make him a well balanced hero.

Luigi

The brother of Mario, Luigi has his own arsenal of combos and moves. From his Up Special to his grab combo, Luigi can deal quick damage to opponents.

Princess Peach

The damsel in distress comes to the battlefield. Using her projectiles and kicks, Peach can be a powerfu character. She even has her float ability that was introduced in Super Mario Bros. 2

Princess Daisy

Even though she has a similar skill set to Princess Peach. She is a more of a tomboy.

Bowser

Mario and Luigi's archnemesis comes to smash to assert his dominance. Using his heavy punches and his fire, he can dominate on the battlefield.

Dr. Mario

The doctor has entered the office. Dr. Mario features a similar moveset to Mario but his recovery is a little questionable. But, he has a great arsenal of moves that makes him a balanced fighter.

Rosalina & Luma

The daughter of Luigi(according to Game Theory), has entered the fight. Rosalina can use Luma for attacks and protection, making her reach farther than the average fighter.

Bowser Jr.

The son of Bowser comes in his ittle Clown Car to reak havoc upon other fighters. The clown car allows Bowser Jr. to send projectiles and get up close for a close combat fight.
