Joker

Based off the Persona 5 anime, Joker makes his entrance into Smash. Joker can use guns for long-range fights while also using counters and absorbs to charge up arsene. When Joker uses arsene, he gets a power boost in all attacks but arsene only is activated for a certain amount of time.

Piranha Plant

One of the enemies Mario encounters has come to take revenge on the Mario Squad. His attacks feature projectiles and a long range side B. This character is a disappointment for a DLC reveal but it is a fascinating character to play.

Banjo & Kazooie

These two may make a better combo than Duck Hunt Dog. Bajo has projectiles and also has a strong side B to knock out opponents. He comes with grenades and eggs to throw at his enemies, this could make for good distancing and edge guarding. The side specil is limited to only 5 times per stock and will recharge back up to 5 when you die.

Terry

Terry comes in from the Fatal Fury series. He functions similarly to both Ryu and Ken. He is not very strong in the air, but when you stay grounded, you are in trouble when you fight Terry.

Byleth

Another Fire Emblem character comes into the roster. Byleth is a tank when it comes to offense but very slow and lacking in the mobility department. With the many weapons Byleth can use and the percents they can kill at, they had to balance him/her out somehow.

Hero

Hero from Dragon Quest coes and brings unique abilities to the battlefield. He possess an MP gauge which players can spend on magic abilities. He also comes with a shield and sword. His shield is able to block projectiles when he is standing idle, walkig, or crouching.
